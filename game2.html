Game 2 Review - Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure RPG set in the dystopian Night City, a megacity obsessed with power, glamour, and body modification. Players assume the role of V, a mercenary searching for a unique implant that holds the key to immortality. The game offers expansive freedom of choice in character customization, story progression, and exploration of a visually stunning, highly detailed world.
